Sons of Anarchy

"Sons of Anarchy" is an American television series that blends drama and crime genres. Set in the fictional small town of Charming, California, the show revolves around the lives of the members of the Sons of Anarchy Motorcycle Club (SAMCRO).
The series focuses on the club's dealings in the world of illegal activities, including drug trafficking and arms dealing, while also exploring the personal and familial relationships within the club.

Themes

One of the main themes of the show is loyalty and brotherhood: the bonds between the members of the club and the sacrifices they makes for each other.
Conflict and redemption is another theme within the show the club frequently face moral dilemmas and seek redemption for their actions.
The most important theme in the show: Family and Legacy is what much of the show centers around, mostly with Jax's bio family and with the club.
And the legacy he wants to leave behind.
