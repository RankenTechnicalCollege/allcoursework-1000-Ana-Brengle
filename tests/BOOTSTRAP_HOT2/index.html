This pumpkin coffee cake is made with a delightful blend of ingredients that create a moist and flavorful treat. Key components include fresh pumpkin puree, which adds rich moisture and natural sweetness, and pure maple syrup for a touch of caramel-like flavor. Seasonal spices like ground cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves infuse the cake with warm, comforting aromas. The crumble topping combines brown sugar, flour, and butter, adding a sweet, crunchy texture. A simple vanilla icing drizzled on top brings everything together, making it the perfect autumn indulgence.
